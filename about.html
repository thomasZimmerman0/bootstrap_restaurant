About Us

Since I was young I was fortunate enough to live at the jersey shore, spending most of my days on the water fishing and dreaming of one day owning and operating my own seafood eatery and market. Fast forward to today, I have incorporated the help of my family to make that dream a reality.

Monte's Fish Fry & Seafood Market is a family owned and operated business. Our mission is to build trust and relations with the community and most of all each and every customer who walks through our door, by making available The freshest and finest choice of fresh and locally caught responsibly-sourced seafood Period.

Our goal is to have our customers leave happier then when they came in.

Long time Ocean county residents and avid fisherman ourselves and a couple of us employed at one time or another in restaurants and markets from Manasquan to L.B.I were able to establish long time relationships with local fishing boats owners and seafood purveyors alike to prepare and sell from dock to store the freshest seafood possible. So stop by and give us a try, "One bite and you'll be hooked."
